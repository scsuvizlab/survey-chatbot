How This Conversation Works

About This Exploration

This is a conversational exploration tool that helps you think through the possibilities and challenges of integrating AI into your teaching. Unlike traditional surveys, it adapts to your responses and explores complexity rather than forcing simple answers.

What to Expect

  • Duration: 15-20 minutes depending on how much you want to share
  • Approach: Natural conversation with questions that work in options (like "would you rather see X, Y, or something else?")
  • Your concerns matter: We'll explore pedagogical, ethical, and institutional concerns without judgment
  • No prescriptions: This isn't about telling you what to do - it's about understanding what you need

Topics We'll Cover

  • Your course and what you want students to remember forever
  • What prompted you to explore AI
  • Concerns (academic integrity, learning quality, institutional policy, etc.)
  • Current assessment approaches and how they align with your values
  • What would make AI adoption feel safe and worthwhile
  • Support needs and next steps

You're In Control

  • Answer however feels right: Brief, detailed, "it depends" - all valid
  • Express uncertainty: "I'm not sure" and "it's complicated" are honest answers
  • End anytime: Click "I'm Done" when you're ready
  • Midpoint check: We'll let you know when we're about halfway through

Your Data

  • Everything you share is saved for the NextEd team to review
  • At the end, you'll see a summary to review and approve
  • Cannot resume if you close the window
  • No right or wrong answers - honest reflection is what matters

What This Isn't

  • Not a sales pitch for AI - skepticism is valid and welcome
  • Not problem-solving - you won't get prescribed solutions
  • Not rushed - take the time you need to think and respond
