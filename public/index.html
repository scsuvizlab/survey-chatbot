How This Works

About This Tool

This is a conversational feedback tool that uses AI (Claude by Anthropic) to gather your thoughts about the VizLab AI Workshop in a more natural, exploratory way than traditional surveys.

What to Expect

  • Duration: About 5-10 minutes
  • Format: Natural conversation with follow-up questions
  • Your answers: Can be any length - detailed, brief, "I don't know," or "I'd rather not say" are all valid
  • Timer: You'll see elapsed time in the top-left. After 10 minutes, the bot will remind you of the time

Your Data

  • The conversation is saved for the NextEd team to review
  • At the end, you'll see a summary to review and approve
  • Sessions can't be resumed - if you close this window, you'd need to start over

How to End

  • Click "I'm Done" at any time to wrap up the conversation
  • The bot will generate a summary for you to review
  • You can approve it or go back to add more

Tips

  • Be as honest and detailed (or brief) as you want
  • The bot won't offer solutions - it's here to listen and understand
  • Press Enter for new lines, click Send to submit
  • If you want to move on from a topic, just say so
