How C3 Works

About This Conversation

C3 explores how principles of student agency, creative control, and authentic voice can apply across ALL disciplines - not just arts. We're interested in where students already exercise creativity in your course, and where AI might amplify (or threaten) that.

What to Expect

  • Duration: 15-20 minutes typically
  • Tone: Collaborative thinking partner, not prescriptive advisor
  • Questions: Designed to surface your thinking, not judge your answers
  • Provocations: We'll offer examples and tensions to explore
  • Resumption: You can pause anytime and pick up later - just login with your email

Topics We'll Explore

  • What you want students to remember forever
  • Where students currently have creative agency
  • The difference between learning and task completion
  • Where AI might amplify student creativity
  • Where AI might bypass important learning
  • Your course context and constraints
  • What you'd need to explore AI safely

You're In Control

  • Answer however feels right - brief, detailed, "it depends" - all valid
  • Express uncertainty - "I'm not sure" is honest
  • Click "I'm Done" whenever you're ready to wrap up
  • Pause and resume anytime - your conversation is saved

What This Isn't

  • Not a sales pitch - skepticism is welcome
  • Not problem-solving - we won't prescribe solutions
  • Not rushed - take the time you need
  • Not anonymous - but your conversation is private to the NextEd team

Technical Notes

  • We'll check in every 10 minutes about time
  • When bot thinks it has covered main topics, you'll get option to dig deeper or wrap up
  • Press Enter for new lines, click Send to submit
  • If stuck, the bot might offer a rating question to keep things moving
